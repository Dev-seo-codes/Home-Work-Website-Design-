Announcement to Increase Investment in Assam

The government has announced a major initiative to boost investment in Assam. This new plan aims to create employment opportunities, support local businesses, and strengthen infrastructure across the state. Officials expect significant economic growth as industries and startups benefit from the incentives and policy reforms.

Government Investment to Create New Jobs in Assam

The Assam government has announced a new industrial package to boost investment in the state. According to the Chief Minister, the plan is expected to create nearly 50,000 jobs over the next five years. The initiative aims to strengthen the state’s economy while promoting local industries and supporting the vision of a self-reliant Assam.
